About the Campaign

Everything you need to know about our Kickstarter

Archfossil is more than a podcast — it's a living, breathing world waiting to be explored at your own table. Our Kickstarter campaign will fund the creation of a full tabletop RPG sourcebook, complete with original lore, maps, character options, and adventure modules set in the world you've come to love.

From the crumbling monoliths of the Shattered Expanse to the volcanic depths of the Molten Vaults, every corner of this world has been crafted with care. Now we need your help to bring it all together in one beautiful, professionally produced book.
