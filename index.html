About the Show

An actual-play TTRPG experience like no other

⚔

Archfossil is a tabletop role-playing adventure brought to life by a passionate group of storytellers, world-builders, and dice-rollers. Every session is an improvised saga of ancient mysteries, unlikely heroes, and choices that ripple across civilizations.

Follow along as our players navigate treacherous ruins, broker fragile alliances, and uncover secrets buried for millennia. Whether you're a seasoned TTRPG veteran or brand-new to the hobby, Archfossil offers a welcoming table where every story matters.

Catch every episode on our podcast, join the conversation on social media, and — if you're feeling bold — sign up for our mail-in campaign and become part of the story yourself.

The World of Archfossil

A realm forged by time, ruin, and untamed magic

🏔

The Frostmarch

A vast frozen expanse of wind-scoured tundra and shattered ice fields in the far north. Nomadic tribes and relic-hunters brave its bitter cold, scavenging the bones of empires swallowed by permafrost.

🌄

The Thornweald

An ancient, sprawling forest at the heart of the continent. Its canopy hides forgotten temples, primordial creatures, and groves where autumn never ends — a place untouched since the dawn of the world.

⛪

Ironhaven

A towering fortress-city on the eastern coast, carved from pale stone and iron. Seat of a crumbling theocracy that clings to power through prophecy, trade, and the strength of its great sea-wall.

🔥

The Cradle

Deep in the volcanic south lies a scarred landscape of molten rivers and ancient forges. Here, exiled artificers and forgotten cults tend fires that have burned since before recorded history.
